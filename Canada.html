Canada

  • Basic Overview: Canada is a large North American country known for natural beauty, modern cities, safety, and high quality of life.
  • Continent & Region: North America.
  • Major Cities: Ottawa (capital), Toronto, Vancouver, Montreal, Calgary.
  • Top Attractions: Niagara Falls, Banff National Park, CN Tower, Rocky Mountains, Vancouver Island.
  • Best Time to Visit: June–September (summer); December–February for winter activities.
  • Visa & Entry: Tourist visa or eTA required; passport validity minimum 6 months.
  • Tour Packages: Nature tours, city sightseeing, family trips, honeymoon packages, adventure tours.
  • Cost & Budget: Budget CAD 90–150/day, mid-range CAD 180–300/day, luxury CAD 400+/day.
  • Accommodation & Transport: Hotels, motels, lodges; domestic flights, trains, buses, car rentals.
  • Food, Culture & Safety: Poutine, maple syrup; very safe, multicultural, and tourist-friendly.
