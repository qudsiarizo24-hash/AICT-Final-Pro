Italy

  • Basic Overview: Italy is a European country famous for ancient history, art, fashion, food, and romantic destinations.
  • Continent & Region: Europe — Southern Europe.
  • Major Cities: Rome (capital), Milan, Venice, Florence, Naples.
  • Top Attractions: Colosseum, Vatican City, Leaning Tower of Pisa, Venice canals, Amalfi Coast.
  • Best Time to Visit: April–June and September–October (pleasant weather and fewer crowds).
  • Visa & Entry: Schengen visa required; passport validity minimum 6 months.
  • Tour Packages: Honeymoon tours, cultural tours, city sightseeing, luxury vacations.
  • Cost & Budget: Budget €70–120/day, mid-range €150–250/day, luxury €350+/day.
  • Accommodation & Transport: Hotels, hostels, resorts; trains, metros, buses, taxis.
  • Food, Culture & Safety: Pizza, pasta, gelato; rich traditions and generally safe for tourists.
