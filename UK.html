United Kingdom

  • Basic Overview: The United Kingdom is a developed country known for its rich history, royal heritage, modern cities, and education.
  • Continent & Region: Europe — Western Europe.
  • Major Cities: London (capital), Manchester, Birmingham, Liverpool, Edinburgh.
  • Top Attractions: Big Ben, London Eye, Buckingham Palace, Tower Bridge, Stonehenge.
  • Best Time to Visit: May to September (pleasant weather); December for Christmas tourism.
  • Visa & Entry: Tourist visa required; passport validity minimum 6 months.
  • Tour Packages: City tours, historical tours, student tours, family tours, luxury trips.
  • Cost & Budget: Budget £80–£120/day, mid-range £150–£250/day, luxury £300+/day.
  • Accommodation & Transport: Hotels, hostels, Airbnb; underground trains, buses, taxis.
  • Food, Culture & Safety: Fish & chips, English breakfast; very safe with strong tourist support.
