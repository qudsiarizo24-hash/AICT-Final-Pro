Maldives

  • Basic Overview: Republic of Maldives, a tropical island nation in the Indian Ocean, famous for clear waters, coral reefs, and overwater villas.
  • Continent & Region: Asia — South Asia, Indian Ocean.
  • Top Cities & Islands: Malé (capital), Vaadhoo Island (Sea of Stars), Cocoa Island, Fihalhohi, Dhigurah.
  • Main Attractions: Snorkeling & diving at Hanifaru Bay, Ithaa Undersea Restaurant, Banana Reef, luxury beaches, sunset cruises.
  • Best Time to Visit: December–April (dry season); May–October is off-season with lower prices.
  • Travel Requirements: Free 30-day visa on arrival, passport valid for 6 months, travel insurance recommended.
  • Tour Packages: Honeymoon, family tours, adventure tours, budget tours, and luxury resort packages.
  • Cost & Budget: Budget $60–$120/day, mid-range $300–$600/day, luxury $800+/day.
  • Accommodation & Transport: Guesthouses, 5★ resorts, speedboats, seaplanes, and public ferries.
  • Food, Culture & Safety: Mas huni, garudhiya, Muslim customs, modest dress, very safe for tourists.
