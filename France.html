France

  • Basic Overview: France is one of the world’s most visited countries, famous for romance, art, history, fashion, and cuisine.
  • Continent & Region: Europe — Western Europe.
  • Major Cities: Paris (capital), Marseille, Lyon, Nice, Bordeaux.
  • Top Attractions: Eiffel Tower, Louvre Museum, Palace of Versailles, French Riviera, Mont Saint-Michel.
  • Best Time to Visit: April–June and September–October (mild weather and fewer crowds).
  • Visa & Entry: Schengen visa required; passport validity minimum 6 months.
  • Tour Packages: Honeymoon tours, cultural tours, wine tours, family holidays.
  • Cost & Budget: Budget €70–120/day, mid-range €150–250/day, luxury €350+/day.
  • Accommodation & Transport: Hotels, hostels, resorts; metro, trains, buses, taxis.
  • Food, Culture & Safety: Croissants, cheese, wine; generally safe and tourist-friendly.
