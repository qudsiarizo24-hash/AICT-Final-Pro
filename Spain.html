Spain

  • Basic Overview: Spain is a vibrant European country known for art, history, festivals, and Mediterranean lifestyle.
  • Continent & Region: Europe — Southern Europe.
  • Major Cities: Madrid (capital), Barcelona, Seville, Valencia, Granada.
  • Top Attractions: Sagrada Familia, Alhambra, Park Güell, La Rambla, Ibiza.
  • Best Time to Visit: April–June and September–October (pleasant weather).
  • Visa & Entry: Schengen visa required; passport validity minimum 6 months.
  • Tour Packages: Cultural tours, beach holidays, honeymoon trips, family tours.
  • Cost & Budget: Budget €60–100/day, mid-range €120–220/day, luxury €300+/day.
  • Accommodation & Transport: Hotels, hostels, resorts; metro, trains, buses.
  • Food, Culture & Safety: Paella, tapas, flamenco; generally safe for tourists.
