Turkey

  • Basic Overview: Turkey is a transcontinental country famous for history, culture, food, and beautiful landscapes.
  • Continent & Region: Europe & Asia — Anatolia and Eastern Thrace.
  • Major Cities: Istanbul, Ankara (capital), Antalya, Izmir, Bursa.
  • Top Attractions: Hagia Sophia, Blue Mosque, Cappadocia, Pamukkale, Grand Bazaar.
  • Best Time to Visit: April–June and September–October (mild weather).
  • Visa & Entry: E-visa available for many nationalities; passport validity required.
  • Tour Packages: Honeymoon tours, cultural tours, adventure tours, budget trips, luxury tours.
  • Cost & Budget: Budget $40–$70/day, mid-range $100–$200/day, luxury $300+/day.
  • Accommodation & Transport: Hotels, resorts, guesthouses; metro, buses, domestic flights.
  • Food, Culture & Safety: Kebabs, baklava, Turkish tea; generally safe for tourists.
