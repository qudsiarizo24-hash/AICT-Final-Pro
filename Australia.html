Australia

  • Basic Overview: Australia is a vast country and continent known for unique wildlife, stunning beaches, modern cities, and vibrant outdoor lifestyle.
  • Continent & Region: Oceania.
  • Major Cities: Canberra (capital), Sydney, Melbourne, Brisbane, Perth, Adelaide.
  • Top Attractions: Sydney Opera House, Great Barrier Reef, Uluru, Bondi Beach, Daintree Rainforest.
  • Best Time to Visit: September–November (spring) and March–May (autumn); December–February for summer beach activities.
  • Visa & Entry: Tourist visa required (eVisitor or ETA); passport validity minimum 6 months.
  • Tour Packages: City tours, wildlife adventures, beach vacations, outback exploration, honeymoon trips.
  • Cost & Budget: Budget AUD 80–150/day, mid-range AUD 200–350/day, luxury AUD 400+/day.
  • Accommodation & Transport: Hotels, motels, hostels, lodges; domestic flights, trains, buses, car rentals.
  • Food, Culture & Safety: Vegemite, fresh seafood, multicultural cuisine; generally very safe, friendly locals, tourist-friendly.
