USA

  • Basic Overview: The United States is a large North American country known for diverse landscapes, iconic cities, cultural variety, and world-famous attractions.
  • Continent & Region: North America.
  • Major Cities: Washington, D.C. (capital), New York City, Los Angeles, Chicago, San Francisco, Miami.
  • Top Attractions: Statue of Liberty, Grand Canyon, Yellowstone National Park, Disney World, Times Square, Golden Gate Bridge.
  • Best Time to Visit: April–June and September–November for mild weather; December–February for winter sports in northern states.
  • Visa & Entry: Tourist visa required (ESTA for eligible countries); passport validity minimum 6 months.
  • Tour Packages: City sightseeing, nature tours, family trips, adventure trips, cultural experiences, honeymoon packages.
  • Cost & Budget: Budget USD 100–180/day, mid-range USD 200–350/day, luxury USD 400+/day.
  • Accommodation & Transport: Hotels, motels, hostels; domestic flights, trains, buses, car rentals.
  • Food, Culture & Safety: Burgers, BBQ, multicultural cuisine; generally safe, diverse culture, very tourist-friendly.
