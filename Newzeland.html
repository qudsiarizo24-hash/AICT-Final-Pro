Newzeland

  • Basic Overview: New Zealand is an island country famous for breathtaking landscapes, adventure sports, and peaceful environment.
  • Continent & Region: Oceania — South Pacific Ocean.
  • Major Cities: Wellington (capital), Auckland, Christchurch, Queenstown.
  • Top Attractions: Milford Sound, Hobbiton, Rotorua, Mount Cook, Fiordland National Park.
  • Best Time to Visit: December–February (summer); June–August for snow activities.
  • Visa & Entry: Tourist visa or NZeTA required; passport validity minimum 6 months.
  • Tour Packages: Adventure tours, nature tours, honeymoon packages, family tours.
  • Cost & Budget: Budget NZ$90–150/day, mid-range NZ$180–300/day, luxury NZ$400+/day.
  • Accommodation & Transport: Hotels, lodges, motels; buses, rental cars, domestic flights.
  • Food, Culture & Safety: Seafood, Māori culture; very safe and tourist-friendly country.
