Thailand

  • Basic Overview: Kingdom of Thailand, known for culture, temples, beaches, nightlife, and food.
  • Continent & Region: Asia — Southeast Asia.
  • Major Cities: Bangkok, Chiang Mai, Phuket, Krabi, Pattaya, Ayutthaya.
  • Top Attractions: Grand Palace, Wat Arun, Phi Phi Islands, Similan Islands, floating markets.
  • Best Time to Visit: November–March (best weather); May–October is monsoon season.
  • Top Attractions: Grand Palace, Wat Arun, Phi Phi Islands, Similan Islands, floating markets.
  • Visa & Entry: Tourist visa required, passport validity 6 months, Thailand Digital Arrival Card (TDAC).
  • Tour Packages: Honeymoon, family, adventure, budget, and luxury tours.
  • Cost & Budget: Budget $30–$80/day, mid-range $80–$190/day, luxury $200–$800+/day.
  • Accommodation & Transport: Hotels, resorts, hostels, trains, buses, tuk-tuks, Grab rides.
  • Food, Culture & Safety: Pad Thai, Tom Yum, respect temples, tourist police available (1155).
