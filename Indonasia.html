Indonasia

  • Basic Overview: Indonesia is a vast island nation known for tropical beaches, volcanoes, rich culture, and biodiversity.
  • Continent & Region: Asia — Southeast Asia.
  • Major Cities: Jakarta (capital), Bali (Denpasar), Surabaya, Bandung, Yogyakarta.
  • Top Attractions: Bali beaches, Borobudur Temple, Komodo National Park, Mount Bromo, Raja Ampat.
  • Best Time to Visit: May–September (dry season); October–April is wetter.
  • Visa & Entry: Visa on arrival available for many nationalities; passport validity minimum 6 months.
  • Tour Packages: Beach holidays, honeymoon tours, adventure tours, cultural trips.
  • Cost & Budget: Budget $30–60/day, mid-range $80–150/day, luxury $250+/day.
  • Accommodation & Transport: Hotels, villas, resorts; domestic flights, ferries, taxis.
  • Food, Culture & Safety: Nasi goreng, satay; diverse cultures and generally safe for tourists.
